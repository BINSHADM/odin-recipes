Italian sushi

Description

Italian Sushi is a creative fusion dish that combines the presentation style of Japanese sushi with classic Italian flavors. Instead of raw fish and rice, it uses ingredients like cured meats, fresh vegetables, cheese, and seasoned rice or bread to create a unique and flavorful roll.

This dish is perfect as an appetizer or party snack and offers a fun twist on traditional Italian cuisine.

This dish is perfect as an appetizer or party snack and offers a fun twist on traditional Italian cuisine. Italian Sushi is easy to customize with different fillings, making it both versatile and visually appealing.

Ingredients

  • 3 slices prosciutto
  • 15 basil leaves
  • 3 Roma tomatoes, sliced
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 slices provolone cheese
  • balsamic vinegar, as needed

Steps

  1. Lay a piece of parchment on a work surface. Lay 3 slices of prosciutto on the paper, making sure each piece slightly overlaps the other.
  2. Cover prosciutto with basil leaves, about 5 leaves per slice. Layer tomatoes on top of basil. Season with pepper.
  3. Tear provolone cheese into pieces, then lay on top of tomatoes. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar.
  4. Starting from the bottom edge, gently roll prosciutto upwards into a cylinder. Wrap roll in the parchment, compressing it slightly so that it holds together.
  5. Refrigerate the roll until ready to serve. Slice the roll into even pieces and remove parchment. Drizzle with more balsamic vinegar.
