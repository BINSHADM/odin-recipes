Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel, and melted chesse. It is hearty, comforting, and perfect for family meals.

this recipe is simple to follow and delivers a flavorful, homemade lasagna that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Lasgna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta chesse
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parsmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive in a pan and saute onion and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. pour in tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Pepeat layers and finish with mozzarella on top.
  7. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 40 minutes.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
